FAQ

What is Coresi OS?
Coresi OS is a personal wellness tool for behavioral self-understanding. It helps you track daily activities, emotions, and states to discover patterns in your life.

Is Coresi OS a medical app?
No. Coresi OS is a wellness tool. It does not diagnose, treat, or prevent any medical condition. It is not a substitute for professional advice.

How do I delete my data?
Go to Settings in the app. You can request data export or account deletion. You can also email us at hello@coresi.io.

What is a bio-day?
A bio-day is your natural sleep-to-sleep cycle, not a calendar day. Events before 6:00 AM belong to the previous bio-day.
